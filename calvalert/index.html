The Problem

Calving is critical – and too often, calves and cows suffer preventable losses

Manual monitoring is exhausting and ineffective, especially at night when most calvings occur.

Current Approaches: Exhausting & Ineffective

Traditional methods require exhausting 24/7 manual monitoring with round-the-clock staffing, leading to farmer burnout

Nighttime monitoring is particularly challenging yet crucial, as most calvings occur during these hours

Manual monitoring diverts valuable labor resources from other essential farm operations, reducing overall productivity

Existing detection systems lack reliability and precision, often missing critical calving events or giving false alarms

Sensor-based systems are physically invasive for animals, require a device per cow, and are prone to loss or damage

Other available solutions lock farmers into expensive proprietary ecosystems with high maintenance costs

Financial Impact

Labor Costs

The Solution

Introducing CalvAlert

AI-Powered Calving Detection that Saves Lives & Reduces Costs

Our intelligent vision system eliminates exhausting night checks and invasive sensors by providing reliable 24/7 monitoring with real-time alerts when intervention is needed most.

Key Features & Advantages

Our innovative solution combines powerful features with unique advantages

Non-Invasive 24/7 Monitoring

AI vision replaces wearables with continuous coverage and infrared night vision, no animal contact needed

Full-Pen Coverage with One Camera

360° fisheye lens covers the entire pen with a single device

AI-Powered Smart Alerts

Real-time notifications from on-device processing with customizable AI-driven predictions

Smart Calving Insights

Track trends for proactive calf and cow health decisions

Fast ROI & Weatherproof Design

Eliminates overnight labor costs while built for harsh farm environments
